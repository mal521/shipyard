Publish to the Internet

Netlify is a free service that turns your project folder into a live website. Drag your folder, get a URL. That's it.

1
Create a free account at netlify.com (so your site stays up permanently).
2
Go to app.netlify.com/drop
3
Drag your project folder (the one with index.html inside) onto the page.
4
Your site is live instantly at a *.netlify.app URL.
Updating later: Log into Netlify, go to your site's Deploys tab, and drag the updated folder onto the deploy area. Your site updates instantly.
